About Me

Interactive Designer focused on UX/UI and Frontend Development. I am passionate about creating digital experiences that are clear, functional, and human-centered.

Disfruto trabajar de forma colaborativa, iterativa y con atención al detalle, buscando siempre generar valor real tanto para los usuarios como para los proyectos. I enjoy working collaboratively and iteratively, with strong attention to detail, always aiming to generate real value for both users and projects. I design UI through design systems, conduct UX research and user testing, and have experience in fronted development , which allows me to create viable solutions and carry them through to implementation.
